French Toast

Golden, crispy on the outside and soft on the inside, French toast is a classic breakfast favorite that never goes out of style. Made by dipping thick slices of bread in a rich egg and milk mixture, then pan-frying them to perfection, this dish is both comforting and indulgent. Whether topped with a dusting of powdered sugar, a drizzle of maple syrup, or a handful of fresh berries, French toast offers the perfect balance of sweetness and warmth. Ideal for weekend brunches or a cozy morning treat, this easy-to-make recipe is sure to become a go-to in your kitchen.

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Eggs
  • Butter
  • Milk
